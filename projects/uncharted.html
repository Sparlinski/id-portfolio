Uncharted Supply Survival Backpack

Leveling up adventures with next-gen survival backpack gear

Uncharted Survival, based out of Colorado, specializes in survival gear, specifically a backpack survival kit that allows adventurers to carry everything they need to survive in the wilderness in case of an emergency. This includes survival clothing, tools, and sustenance. Uncharted currently has two backpack offerings that contain these survival kits and wanted to explore the next iteration of this product range. As the lead designer on this project, I was tasked with researching and exploring creative solutions for what that may entail.

Ideation And Development

I set out to evaluate the current backpack, focusing on everything from the ergonomics of the backpack, materials used in its contruction, features of the backpack, identify any existing user issues to evaluating the contents of the survival kit. My goal was to find opportunities to enhance the value for the user. What follows is an evolution of the concepts that Uncharted could present to their team for evaluation and continue forward for further development.
