Koozie Sports Bottle

Balancing reliable performance with a touch of fun

As part of Koozie Group's rebranding initiative, they wanted a product offering that catered to a more active lifestyle while retaining their fun aesthetic. My job was to strike a balance in creating a product that invoked Koozie's lighter side while still appealing communication a sense of function and reliability.

Development

Concepts were explored through sketching, communicating form, aesthetics, and features. These concept sketches evolved into CAD models and renderings, which were then evaluated through prototypes, evolving iteration after iteration until the final design could be confidently selected for manufacturing from the iterative phases of the design process.
